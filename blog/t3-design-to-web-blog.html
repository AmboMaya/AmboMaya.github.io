What is a responsive site and why responsiveness is important

A responsive site adjusts the display of the content. The layout of responsive webpage is adjustable based on screen size of device the user uses. The advance of internet technology also changed the way we use internet. We can access it from different environment such as desktop, tablet, smart mobile phones and even television screen. Some of the importances of responsive design are:

  • Almost everyone owns a mobile phones that can access internet
  • Better user experience which means people more likely to visit your site. And for companies this may increase sales lead.
  • According to Destination Next article on the importance of responsive web design, SEO campaigns and website maintenance are also a much smoother process with a mobile friendly website. Responsive web design means you only have 1 website to look after.
What is mobile first design and why it's important

Mobile first design is when the experience is defined on mobile platforms such as smartphones and tablets and then scaled up to larger screens. It's important due to an increasing number of mobile usage.

What are frameworks and their pros and cons

Frameworks are built code that offers customs solutions or functionalities for website properties.

Pros:

  • It saves a lot of time in developments
  • It offers a standardization
  • It offers a lot of sollutions to most problems

Cons:

  • It generally adds more time for your page to render if not done properly
  • In some case, you can loose a little bit of flexibility
  • The owner of frameworks can introduce any new features that you may not necessarily need for your project. This also ties in to the fact that you're locked-in to use the framework once you start your project. You have less choice change to another sollution because you would have invested time to adopt it in the first place. Thus it is important to do a thorough research for framework that best suits your project need.
