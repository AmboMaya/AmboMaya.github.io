Cultural Blog - Leadership


Compassionate Leadership

My personal experience with compassionate leadership leads to feeling happiness and joyful coming to work as subordinate not as the leader. I have also experienced not so compassionate leadership which result in resignation due to so much unhappiness. This book has some realistic points.

Multiplying goodness meditation

You will need to do this meditation with open heart and the right state of mind. I grew up with a religious background in Indonesia and we had a curriculum at school that teaches us about practising good morale, compassion, tolerance, living in a harmonious society, etc. When you were young you don't really know the real meaning of these until you experience them personally in your adult life. I did this somewhere quiet no distraction with a headphone on. The experience was pleasnt. I felt warmth in my chest and feeling peaceful. It may sound a bit exagerating but for me it really helps to take away some negative feelings and judgements towards others.
