empathic listening

I think of myself as a good listener but after reading this section of empathic listening, it changed the way I listen. Listening with mindfulness makes it a lot easier to listen to people sharing their stories, particularly when they're feeling down. Instead of worrying that you're going to get dragged into their sadness, you listen without judging instead with more caring.
